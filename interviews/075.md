Interviewer: When you and your sister are planning to watch something together, how does that actually come together?

Interviewer: How do you normally find out about new shows in the first place?

P: Honestly? People. My friends text me, my family texts me, I see stuff on Instagram. I don't really browse streaming apps to discover things. I just wait for someone to tell me something's good and then I go find it. That's way easier than scrolling through recommendations that don't make sense.

Interviewer: And then once you find it, you just... start watching?

P: Yeah, pretty much. Or I'll add it to a mental list and get to it eventually. I don't have like a system for tracking what I want to watch. I just hope I remember when I have time.
