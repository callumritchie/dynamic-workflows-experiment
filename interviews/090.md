P: Just the back-and-forth, honestly. If I could just see "okay, these are the shows we both have," that would save so much time. Instead I'm like, "I'm opening Netflix... okay, now I'm opening HBO Max..." and she's doing the same thing. We're just comparing screenshots at that point.

P: Not really. I feel like it just keeps creeping up. Like, I remember when Netflix was $9.99 and now it's what, $16? Interviewer: When you do decide to watch something, what does that decision-making process look like?
